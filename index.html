MediTrack Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 31, 2025
Last Updated: May 31, 2025

Important Medical Disclaimer

MediTrack is a medication management tool and is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition or medication regimen. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay in seeking it because of something you have read or used in this app.

In case of a medical emergency, call your local emergency services immediately.

1. Introduction

MediTrack ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address (through Apple Sign In)
  • Health Information: Medication names, dosages, schedules, and adherence records
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app usage analytics

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage, crash reports
  • Technical Data: IP address, device identifiers, app version

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Core Services: Medication reminders, scheduling, and tracking
  • Enable Family Coordination: Sync data between caregiver and patient accounts
  • Send Notifications: Medication reminders and alerts
  • Improve Our App: Analytics to enhance user experience
  • Ensure Security: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell your personal information.

We may share information in these limited circumstances:

  • Between Linked Accounts: Data sharing between caregivers and patients who are connected
  • Service Providers: Firebase/Google Cloud for data storage and sync
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition (with notice)

5. Data Storage and Security

Cloud Storage

  • Firebase/Google Cloud: Your data is stored on Google's secure servers
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication required

Local Storage

  • Core Data: Some data stored locally on your device for offline access
  • Security: Protected by iOS security features and device passcode

6. Your Rights and Choices

Account Controls

  • Access: View your personal information in the app
  • Update: Modify your medication information and account details
  • Delete: Remove medications, logs, or delete your entire account
  • Data Portability: Export your medication data

Communication Preferences

  • Notifications: Control medication reminders and alerts in app settings
  • Marketing: We do not send marketing communications

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data while your account is active
  • Account Deletion: Data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law
  • Aggregated Data: Anonymous usage statistics may be retained indefinitely

8. Children's Privacy

MediTrack is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information.

10. Third-Party Services

Firebase/Google Cloud Platform

  • Service: Data storage, authentication, and real-time sync
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Data Processing: Governed by Google's data processing terms

Apple Sign In

  • Service: Authentication
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/
  • Data Minimization: Apple provides minimal data to protect your privacy

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes through:

  • In-App Notification: Prominent notice in the app
  • Email: If we have your email address
  • App Store: Updated policy will be available with app updates

12. Health Information Notice

HIPAA Compliance

MediTrack is a personal health management tool and is not a HIPAA-covered entity. We do not transmit your information to healthcare providers unless you specifically choose to do so.

Data Accuracy

You are responsible for the accuracy of medication information entered into the app. Always verify medication details with your healthcare provider or pharmacist.

Emergency Situations

MediTrack is not designed for emergency situations. In case of medical emergency, contact emergency services immediately.

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know: What personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Non-Discrimination: Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights

14. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access: Obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Object: Object to processing of your personal data
